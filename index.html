About Dr Purushottam

Dr Purushottam Bhandare is a policy researcher and data analyst specializing in public policy, financial inclusion, and social entrepreneurship. With a Ph.D. in Management and Labor Studies from the Tata Institute of Social Sciences and an MA in Public Policy from the University of Chicago, Purushottam brings a unique blend of academic rigor and practical experience to his work.

His research interests span public policy, education, inequality, financial inclusion, behavioral science, technology, healthcare, climate change, and sustainable development goals. Purushottam has authored multiple peer-reviewed articles and presented his findings at international conferences, contributing significantly to the fields of financial literacy and social entrepreneurship.

With experience ranging from leading quantitative research studies to analyzing complex datasets, Purushottam excels in translating data into actionable policy recommendations. His work has impacted various sectors, including banking, urban development, and social welfare.

Currently serving as a Senior Peace Fellow at the Peace Economy Project, Purushottam continues to leverage his analytical skills and interdisciplinary background to drive positive change through effective policy solutions.
