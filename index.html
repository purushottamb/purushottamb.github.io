Policy Researcher & Data Analyst

Dr. Purushottam Bhandare

Purushottam is a policy researcher and data analyst. His expertise spans public policy, financial inclusion, and social entrepreneurship, with research focusing on education, inequality, healthcare, and sustainable development.

He has authored multiple peer-reviewed publications and presented at international conferences. Currently serving as a Senior Peace Fellow, Purushottam translates complex data into actionable policy recommendations to drive social impact.
